About Malaysia....


Malaysia is a country in Southeast Asia. The 13 states and 3 federal territories that make up the federal constitutional monarchy are seperated into penisular Malaysia and East Borneo by the South China Sea. Penisular Malaysia shares marine boundaries with Singapore, Vietnam, and Indonesia in addition to land ans sea borders with Thailand.

East Malaysia shares marine borders with Vietnam and the Philippines, as well as land and sea borders with Brunei and Indonesia. The largest city in the nation and the home of legislative branch of the federal government is Kuala Lumpur. The nation is the 43rd most populous in the world, with a population of more than 33 million.

Malaysian's population is diversified, comprising Malays, Chinese, Indians, and people with indigenous ancestry. It resenbles a melting pot of cinilizations. Malay is the Malaysia's native tongue. English is still a widely used second language. It is evident that diversity has little influence on Malaysian culture. Modernity and tradition are amazing blended in Malaysia's rich and deverse past. The culture and traditions of the people are what essentially shape their way of life and attract tourists from all over the world.
